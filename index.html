Smart Fit System

A Trusted Online Clothing Fit and Order Management Platform

About the Project

Smart Fit System is a trusted online clothing fit and order management platform designed to improve confidence in online fashion shopping.

The platform helps customers find the correct clothing size by using body measurements such as height, weight, waist, hips, and bust measurements.

It also connects buyers with verified clothing vendors while providing secure order tracking, personalized recommendations, and transparent communication throughout the shopping experience.

Key Features

User Registration

Create an account and manage your personal profile securely.

Body Measurements

Enter height, weight, waist, hips, and bust measurements for accurate sizing.

Size Recommendations

Receive personalized clothing size recommendations based on your measurements.

Verified Sellers

Purchase from trusted clothing vendors with verified business profiles.

Order Tracking

Track your order from purchase to delivery in real time.

Customer Reviews

Read and share reviews to help build trust within the marketplace.

Fit Finder

Enter your body measurements to receive a personalized clothing size recommendation.
