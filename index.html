Smart Fit System

A Trusted Online Clothing Fit and Order Management Platform

About the Project

Smart Fit System is a trusted online clothing fit and order management platform designed to improve confidence in online fashion shopping.

The platform helps customers find the correct clothing size by using body measurements such as height, weight, waist, hips, and bust measurements.

It also connects buyers with verified clothing vendors while providing secure order tracking, personalized recommendations, and transparent communication throughout the shopping experience.
